IRON - MAN

Iron Man is the superhero persona of Anthony Edward "Tony" Stark,
a businessman and engineer who runs the weapons manufacturing
company Stark Industries. When Stark was captured in a war zone
and sustained a severe heart wound, he built his Iron Man armor
and escaped his captors.

History

  • Character Origins

    Iron Man debuted in 1963 in *Tales of Suspense* #39, created by Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, Don Heck, and Jack Kirby. His origin story tells of an inventor and industrialist who, injured during a conflict, builds an armor to save his life and fight crime.

    His character explores the connection between technology and responsibility, addressing moral themes related to the power of weapons and the defense of good.

    View More

  • Tony Stark

    Tony Stark, alias Iron Man, is a brilliant billionaire and inventor in the Marvel universe. Initially a weapons manufacturer, he changes his ways after being kidnapped, creating a suit of armor to survive and later to fight crime. Charismatic but tormented, he faces enemies like the Mandarin and struggles with his own weaknesses, including alcoholism.

    A key member of the Avengers, he plays crucial roles in events like Civil War and ultimately sacrifices himself in Avengers: Endgame.

    View More

  • The Stark Industries

    Stark Industries is a fictional company in the Marvel universe, founded by Howard Stark and later led by his son, Tony Stark. Initially a weapons manufacturer, the company transforms under Tony’s leadership, shifting towards more ethical technological innovations, including scientific and medical projects.

    Stark Industries becomes a symbol of technological progress, with Tony's inventions, including the Iron Man suit, helping to improve society.

    View More

  • The Wife

    Pepper Potts, portrayed by Gwyneth Paltrow, is Tony Stark's wife in the Marvel universe. Initially Tony's assistant, she becomes a key figure in both his personal and professional life. She is intelligent, pragmatic, and empathetic, balancing Tony's impulsiveness.

    Their relationship develops throughout the films, with Pepper supporting him during difficult times. In Avengers: Endgame she takes on the role of Rescue, fighting alongside Tony.

    View More

  • The Actor

    Robert Downey Jr. is an actor and producer best known for his role as Iron Man in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. Born in 1965, he began his career at a young age, gaining recognition with Chaplin (1992), but faced struggles with drug addiction that nearly ended his career.

    After rehabilitation, he made a stunning comeback with Iron Man (2008), becoming a global superstar. Beyond the MCU, he starred in Sherlock Holmes and other major films.

2008's Iron Man tells the story of Tony Stark, a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor who is kidnapped and forced to build a devastating weapon. Instead, using his intelligence and ingenuity, Tony builds a high-tech suit of armor and escapes captivity.
